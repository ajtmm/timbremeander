# “will the world die a little”

*by [Halina Poświatowska](https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poets/halina-poswiatowska)*
*Translated from the Polish*

will the world die a little\
when I die

I watch him going going\
dressed in a fox collar

I could never be\
a thread in that scarf

I was always here\
he wasn’t

and yet\
it’s nice to think\
the world will die a little\
when I die
