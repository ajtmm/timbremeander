# We Real Cool

*by [Gwendolyn Brooks](https://poets.org/poet/gwendolyn-brooks)*

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;THE POOL PLAYERS. \
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;SEVEN AT THE GOLDEN SHOVEL.

We real cool. We\
Left school. We

Lurk late. We\
Strike straight. We

Sing sin. We\
Thin gin. We

Jazz June. We\
Die soon.
