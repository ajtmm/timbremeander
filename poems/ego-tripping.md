# Ego Tripping (there may be a reason why)
*by [Nikki Giovanni](https://poets.org/poet/nikki-giovanni)*

I was born in the congo\
I walked to the fertile crescent and built\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;the sphinx\
I designed a pyramid so tough that a star\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;that only glows every one hundred years falls\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;into the center giving divine perfect light\
I am bad

I sat on the throne\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;drinking nectar with allah\
I got hot and sent an ice age to europe\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;to cool my thirst\
My oldest daughter is nefertiti\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;the tears from my birth pains\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;created the nile\
I am a beautiful woman

I gazed on the forest and burned\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;out the sahara desert\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;with a packet of goat’s meat\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;and a change of clothes\
I crossed it in two hours\
I am a gazelle so swift\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;so swift you can’t catch me

For a birthday present when he was three\
I gave my son hannibal an elephant\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;He gave me rome for mother’s day\
My strength flows ever on

My son noah built new/ark and\
I stood proudly at the helm\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;as we sailed on a soft summer day\
I turned myself into myself and was\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;jesus\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;men intone my loving name\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;All praises All praises\
I am the one who would save

I sowed diamonds in my back yard\
My bowels deliver uranium\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;the filings from my fingernails are\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;semi-precious jewels\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;On a trip north\
I caught a cold and blew\
My nose giving oil to the arab world\
I am so hip even my errors are correct\
I sailed west to reach east and had to round off\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;the earth as I went\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;The hair from my head thinned and gold was laid\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;across three continents

I am so perfect so divine so ethereal so surreal\
I cannot be comprehended\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;except by my permission

I mean . . . I . . . can fly\
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;like a bird in the sky . . .
